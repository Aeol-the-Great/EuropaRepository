# 📊 Europa Data Sheet (For Science Project)

| Field | Data |
|---|---|
| **Size compared to Earth** | ~1/4 the diameter of Earth (Equatorial diameter: 1,940 miles). |
| **Composition of surface** | Predominantly water ice; smooth with dark, reddish-brown cracks (salts/sulfur). |
| **Atmosphere** | Extremely tenuous molecular oxygen (Surface pressure 100 billionx less than Earth). |
| **Temperature** | -260°F (-160°C) average; poles drop to -370°F. |
| **Location in solar system** | Orbits Jupiter (5.2 AU from the Sun); 2nd closest Galilean moon to Jupiter. |
| **Gravity** | 13.4% of Earth's (A 100lb person would weigh **14 lbs**). |
| **Orbit (Length of 1 year)** | 3.55 Earth days (Time to orbit Jupiter). |
| **Rotation (Length of 1 day)** | 3.55 Earth days (Tidally locked; one side always faces Jupiter). |
| **Satellites (Moons) & Rings** | Europa has no moons or rings; but its' parent Jupiter has 95 moons and a faint ring system. |
| **Travel time from Earth** | ~6 years (based on Europa Clipper mission). |
| **Additional Fact 1** | Europa's subterranean ocean may hold **twice as much liquid water** as all of Earth's oceans combined. |
| **Additional Fact 2** | The "Liedenbrock Sea" (user-proposed) would exist under an ice shell that is likely 10–15 miles (15–25 km) thick. |

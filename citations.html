Classified Expedition Intelligence: Moon EUROPA

Physical Profile

Diameter: ~3,100 km (About 1/4 the size of Earth). Almost exactly the size of Earth's Moon.

Surface: Water Ice & Salts. One of the smoothest objects in the solar system.

Atmosphere & Temp

Air: Thin Oxygen atmosphere. Not breathable, but biologically significant.

Temp: -260°F (-160°C) at the equator. Deadly surface cold protects the ocean below.

Orbital Mechanics

Distance: 670,900 km from Jupiter. Constantly flexed by Jupiter's massive gravity.

Orbit: 3.55 Earth Days. Synchronously locked with Jupiter.

The Liedenbrock Sea

Ocean: Saltwater Liquid. Contains more water than all of Earth's oceans combined.

Life Potential: Hydrothermal Vents. High chance of chemo-synthetic life near the floor.
